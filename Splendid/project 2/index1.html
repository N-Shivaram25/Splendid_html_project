Room Types

Single Occupancy Room

A room assigned to one person.May have one or more beds. The room-size or area of Single Rooms are generally between 37 m^2 to 100 m^2.

Family Room

A parlour or living room connected with to one or more bedrrooms .(A room width one or more bedrooms and a separate living space.)

Duplex room

A duplex room is a type of accommodation featuring two levels connected by an internal staircase. Typically, the lower level consists of a living area, while the upper level contains sleeping quarters. It offers more space and privacy compared to standard hotel rooms, making it ideal for families or longer stays.
